| T-17-05 | I (Information Disclosure) | Hostnames + InstallLocation values in committed markdown | accept | Hostnames follow the Master Electronics naming convention (CITY-DEPT-### / CITY-SERIAL-COMPANY) and are already present in every HTML/JSON report SCRY generates.
